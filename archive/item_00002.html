Daugerotype of TP Eschewing Acolyte

Anderson Baker, Acolyte, Daytona Beach chapter.

Baker has submitted this daugerotype as a relic for authentication by the Beloved. His meditation upon the relic was accompanied by visions of swiss cheese, said to symbolize the porous nature of temporal consciousness. Some followers remain skeptical that Baker will attain Knight Temporal status given his infamous renunciation of toilet paper, a source of contention within the commune.
